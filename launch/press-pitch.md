# Spanish tech-press micro-pitch

**Targets:** Genbeta, Xataka, El Output (and any autónomo/fintech newsletter).
**Angle:** novelty = newsworthy → "el primer servidor MCP fiscal de España". One short paragraph + demo. Personalize the outlet name; don't mass-blast.

---

## Email — subject lines (pick one)
- `El primer servidor MCP fiscal de España: la IA ya puede consultar tu IAE/CNAE con datos oficiales`
- `He conectado los datos fiscales de la AEAT a la IA (MCP) — os lo cuento por si encaja`

> Hola [nombre]:

>
> Más info: https://www.conversoriaecnae.es/mcp · Repo: https://github.com/conversoriaecnae/mcp

> Brian Mena Gómez · brian@conversoriaecnae.es

## Notes
- Lead with the news hook (first-of-category), not the product.
- Offer ready-made assets (lowers their effort = more likely to publish).
- Keep it to one screen; attach nothing, link everything.
